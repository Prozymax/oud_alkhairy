About Our Mining Engagement

Strategic Mining Partnership

We are engaged in a strategic partnership within the mining sector. Our collaboration ensures reliable sourcing, operational support, and smooth material procurement processes that meet international standards.

Through our agreement with our mining partner, we have access to high-end quality raw and processed materials sourced directly from verified mining operations.

Our goal is to create long-term, mutually beneficial partnerships in the mining industry, connecting quality material sources with global markets.

We ensure transparency, quality assurance, and reliable delivery timelines for all our mining material supplies.

Our Materials Portfolio

High-Quality Mining Materials

We supply a comprehensive range of premium raw and processed mining materials, sourced directly from our mining partners with full quality certification and traceability.

Wolframite

Chemical Formula: (Fe, Mn) WO₄

Primary ore of tungsten, essential for high-temperature applications, steel alloys, and industrial manufacturing.

Scheelite

Chemical Formula: CaWO₄

Calcium tungstate mineral, another important tungsten ore used in metallurgy and industrial applications.

Cassiterite/Tin

Chemical Formula: SnO₂

Primary ore of tin, widely used in electronics, soldering, and coating applications.

Lead (Pb)

Element: Pb

Essential metal for batteries, radiation shielding, and various industrial applications.

Zinc (Zn)

Element: Zn

Critical metal for galvanization, alloys, and chemical manufacturing processes.

Fluorite

Chemical Formula: CaF₂

Calcium fluoride mineral used in steel production, ceramics, and chemical industries.

Silver (Ag)

Element: Ag

Precious metal for electronics, jewelry, and industrial applications with excellent conductivity.

Monazite

Rare Earth Mineral

Phosphate mineral containing rare earth elements, crucial for modern technology applications.

Copper (Cu)

Element: Cu

Essential metal for electrical wiring, electronics, construction, and renewable energy systems.

Tantalite

Chemical Formula: Ta₂O₆

Primary source of tantalum, critical for electronics, capacitors, and aerospace applications.
